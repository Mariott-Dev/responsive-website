HOW IT WORKS

You could argue that "waterjet" is a bit of a misnomer, as the reality is that an abrasive is used to cut materials in addition to pressurized water. This is a bit like highly focused sandblasting with a water component. Existing templates or specialty designs are entered into the waterjet's computer and precision cuts are executed by the machine with respect to the thickness and composition of the material being cut. S&S Waterjet is an approximately 6x8 foot cut surface with a 2D axis.

STEEL CUTTING

One of the amazing things about waterjet cutting is an ability to cut sensitive materials without warping and flammable materials without burning. Portable waterjet machines are often used to cut reactors out of refineries without setting them ablaze. It is also important to note that waterjet tables are not inhibited by metallurgy the way that cutting torches are. All alloys and mild steels can be cut by waterjet regardless of composition as opposed to the limits and distortions of torches.

STONE & COMPOSITES

City, county, and company signage stones are regularly cut by S&S Welding. Stones can be cut clean through and backlit or steel templates can be cut and placed over the stones to allow controlled depth sandblasting and painting. Granite counter tops and specialty composites for any manner of application can be designed and cut by our waterjet table. Concretes, tiles, bricks, slate, and heavy plastics for civil construction have all been handled regardless of properties and specific considerations.
