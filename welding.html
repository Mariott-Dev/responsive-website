TIG WELDING

S&S Welding has a wealth of experience with heliarc/tig welding from purged alloy process piping in industrial plants to mechanical grade aluminum sand hauling tankers. S&S has welded alloys ranging from high nickel-chromoly content piping like Inconel to low carbon content stainless like 304 and 316. Special attention to heat sensitive material and penetration requirements are fully understood here. Drilling Kellys requiring full penetration with zero internal bead tolerance are tig welded regularly.

MIG WELDING

High volume, high tensile strength dual shield welding is an S&S specialty. S&S Welding has designed and fabricated tong truck crane beds, lay-down machines, bucket rigs, track rigs, drilling buckets, core barrels, over-the-road winch beds, chain racks, tool boxes, motor houses, pump sheds, electrical skids, pipe racks/tubs, dog houses, rig derricks, tail rolls, wind walls, cement mixers, separator skids, open-top tanks, and much more. Hard-facing, pad-welding, and fish-plating are also regular occurrences.

STICK WELDING

S&S Welding has performed traditional stick welding in virtually every venue imaginable, beginning with API 1104 x-ray and destructive testing for industrial plant work and ending with branch saddle Tee x-ray and destructive testing for oilfield pipeline and well location construction. Refineries, paper mills, power plants, chemical facilities, compressor stations, well locations, pipelines, gathering lines, rig maintenance, machine repair, bridge construction, and civil project have all been tackled by S&S.
